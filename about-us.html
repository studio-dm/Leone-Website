SPECIALTY

My specialty is in the valuation – marketing – negotiation – and sale of Florida Businesses. My process involves confidentially working with business owners, while they continue to focus on running their business. I have been involved in the sale of “DME’s” (Durable medical equipment) -Detoxes-Assisted Living Facility-Ace Hardware-Build your own boat e-commerce company- Roofing Co.-Insurance Agency, and the list goes on. With 30 years of practical business experience & ownership, I can help both buyer & seller come to a fair deal.

I like to consider myself a true “Main Street Broker”, who understands what it takes to own and operate a small business. I have also previously sold: Pet grooming, Florist, Grocery stores, Daycare centers, Music academy, Tanning Salon, Hair & Nail salon, Golf store, Medical Chair rehabilitation, Laundromat, as well as a long list of Florida restaurants & bars. So, when it comes to the purchase or sale of a Florida businesses, I am the guy to turn to.
