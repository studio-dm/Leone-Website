How to Search Florida Business Listings

You may view or search the Business Brokers of Florida Multi-List system (MLS) by using the links on this page.

  • Featured Listings are the businesses listed for sale by Acquisition Experts LLC.
  • Search Florida Businesses for Sale will allow you to search all businesses for sale in the entire State using a variety of criteria such as County, type of business, gross or net revenues, etc.  Click here for detailed instructions.

Quick Search Links:

  • Treasure Coast Listings are the businesses for sale in Martin, St. Lucie, Indian River and Brevard counties.
  • Million Dollar PLUS Businesses for Sale – Florida east coast businesses with an asking price greater than $1,000,000.
  • Lender Pre-Qualified Businesses for Sale – Florida east coast businesses that have been pre-qualified for a SBA 7(a) loan.
  • VISA Qualified Businesses for Sale – Florida east coast businesses that should qualify for an E2 investor visa.
  • Restaurants for Sale on Florida’s east coast.
  • Lawn/Landscape Businesses for Sale on Florida’s east coast.

Contact us with the listing numbers of the ones which interest you. Be prepared to sign a Confidentiality Disclosure and provide some indication of your financial ability to buy a business. For more information on preparing to buy a business, please view our Buyer’s Guide section on this web site.
