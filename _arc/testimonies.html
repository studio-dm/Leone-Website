What my clients are saying:

To: Bob Leone

When we were in the market to purchase our restaurant, Bob Leone sold it to us. When the unthinkable happened and tragedy struck, he stood by us and sold it for us.

Those two sentences don't tell the whole story - they can't. Mr. Leone is a dedicated, honest salesman with a wealth of business knowledge, sales experience, integrity, honesty and not a small amount of heart. He believes in the product he sells - otherwise he wouldn't be half as good at what he does - but he also believes in the people behind each transaction. He pays attention to his clients' needs whether they're potential buyers or hopeful sellers, frequently understanding his clients' needs far better than they themselves do.

Let me now explain what I mean by "tragedy struck," and then explain why we are so grateful.

The job market affected us as it affected so many other people, and since John had ample restaurant management and operations experience he started looking for businesses in that sector. His first plan was to start his own, but the economic situation at the time wasn't conducive to small business loans regardless of experience and a good business plan. So, he began to hunt for the perfect little restaurant with its established customer base. He found such a restaurant being brokered by Mr. Leone.

About a month after his happy purchase, he became ill and sadly, within two months, he passed away. My mother then found herself as the sole owner of a restaurant while also happily employed in the public sector and without any time to dedicate to run a restaurant. She understandably decided to sell, contacting Bob as soon as she felt able. She explained the situation to Mr. Leone, who was more than happy to help. I ran the restaurant while it was on the market, liaising with Bob when he needed information from us or had an interested buyer seeking the grand tour.

It was a difficult time in our lives. Much to our surprise, the sale was absolutely not a source of stress for us, and that's entirely due to the way Mr. Leone conducts his business. He's personable, honest, and he only contacted us to keep us up to date or request information. Not once did he make us feel pressured and, indeed, was more a source of strength and reliability than anything else.
